Chicken Cottage

Chicken cottage is an American fast food company, founded in 1940 as a restaurant operated by Muhammad Toqeer, in San Bernardino, California, United States. They rechristened their business as a hamburger stand, and later turned the company into a franchise, with the Golden Arches logo being introduced in 1953 at a location in Phoenix, Arizona.
